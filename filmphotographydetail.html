35mm & Medium Format Stills

The Night Shift Series

This series explores the moody atmosphere and isolated beauty of urban spaces after midnight. All images were captured on Portra 400 film, pushing the stock to its limit under available street light to achieve a distinctive, grainy, and high-contrast look.

Gear: Leica M6, Hasselblad 500C/M | Film: Kodak Portra 400 (Pushed 1 stop)
